About This Project

We are a team of Computer Science students using our knowledge in technology and statistics to create this meaningful learning tool. This website was developed as part of our Advanced Statistics course, which focuses on Hypothesis Testing. It aims to help students and learners understand statistical concepts in a more interactive and accessible way.


Through this project, we combine programming, data analysis, and design to build a platform that supports learning and exploration of hypothesis testing in both theory and application.
